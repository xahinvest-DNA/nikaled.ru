# Shotlist

Format: `9:16`, realistic, light comedy, soft daylight, clean small-town street, no cartoon look.

## Shot 1 - Confident approach

Duration: `3-4 sec`

Goal: establish the serious orange cat walking like an important client.

Prompt:

```text
Realistic vertical shot, small-town street in daylight, clean storefronts, soft natural light. A fluffy orange cat walks confidently along the sidewalk like an important customer. Head high, serious face, businesslike attitude, slightly arrogant expression, elegant steady pace. Cinematic realism, detailed fur, natural motion, no people in close-up, no cartoon style.
```

On-screen text:

`Когда у бизнеса нет нормальной вывески...`

## Shot 2 - Confusion at the door

Duration: `4-5 sec`

Goal: cat notices a plain door with a tiny faded sign and becomes confused.

Prompt:

```text
Realistic vertical shot, same orange cat stops at a plain shop door with no proper storefront sign, only a tiny faded plate on the door that is almost invisible. The cat looks left, right, up, then back at the door. Facial expression changes from confidence to confusion and mild irritation. Subtle comedic timing, realistic whiskers, natural head tilt, clean facade, soft daylight, professional ad style.
```

On-screen text:

`Клиент ищет вас`

`Клиент не находит вас`

## Shot 3 - Judging look to camera

Duration: `3-4 sec`

Goal: cat walks past, stops, and slowly turns to camera with offended disbelief.

Prompt:

```text
Realistic vertical commercial shot. The fluffy orange cat takes two steps past the unclear shop entrance, suddenly stops, and slowly turns its head toward the camera with an expression of offended disbelief, as if saying: how was I supposed to find this place. Brief comedic pause, slightly widened eyes, twitching whiskers, realistic anatomy, cinematic framing, no absurdity.
```

On-screen text:

`Клиент немного осуждает`

## Shot 4 - Sign reveal and approval

Duration: `4-5 sec`

Goal: same storefront now has a strong readable sign, cat approves and enters.

Prompt:

```text
Realistic vertical commercial shot of the same storefront, now upgraded with a bright stylish readable sign on the facade, clean modern design, illuminated letters, clear business branding, attractive and visible from a distance. The fluffy orange cat instantly notices the sign, becomes satisfied, gives a small approving nod like an expert, and calmly walks inside. Soft daylight, clean realistic street, polished advertising look.
```

On-screen text:

`А потом появляется нормальная наружная реклама`

## Shot 5 - Final hero frame

Duration: `2-3 sec`

Goal: final branding shot with visible sign and happy cat near entrance.

Prompt:

```text
Realistic vertical final commercial frame. Beautiful storefront sign in clear view, clean facade, good daylight, strong readable branding. The fluffy orange cat sits proudly near the entrance looking satisfied and calm. Light humorous atmosphere, premium outdoor advertising feel, clean composition, realistic details, no extra animals, no messy background.
```

On-screen text:

`Сделаем так, чтобы вас находили без квеста`

## Global negative prompt

```text
deformed paws, extra eyes, broken cat anatomy, scary face, aggressive behavior, dirty storefront, unreadable sign, warped letters, cartoon style, fantasy elements, shaky camera, blur, low detail, extra animals, people in close-up, chaotic motion
```
